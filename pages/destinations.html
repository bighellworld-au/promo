Australian Destinations & Regions

Overview of popular region types and planning basics.

Coastal Regions

Extensive coastlines with beaches, reef areas, and port towns. Plan coastal drives and day trips.

The Outback

Arid interior landscapes; prepare for remote travel and check weather.

Rainforests & Tropics

Wet tropics with diverse flora and fauna; stay on marked paths.

Wine & Food Regions

Vineyards and produce trails are popular weekend activities.

National Parks

Protected ecosystems and heritage. Review alerts and permitted activities.

Islands & Marine Life

Islands and reserves offer snorkeling and wildlife viewing.

Alpine Areas

Cooler climates and seasonal activities; weather changes quickly.

Lakes & Rivers

Boating, fishing, and trails — respect regulations.

Heritage Towns

Museums, tours, and historic architecture.
