NeoAus Guide Privacy Policy

At NeoAus Guide, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how it is used, and the steps we take to ensure it is kept secure.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information when you visit our website, subscribe to our newsletter, or interact with our services. The information we collect may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Technical data, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Information about how you interact with our website (e.g., pages visited, time spent on the site, etc.)

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for various purposes, including:

  • To provide you with the services you request (e.g., sending newsletters or updates)
  • To improve the content and functionality of our website
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To monitor and analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • To communicate important updates about our site or services

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us analyze website traffic, personalize content, and improve functionality.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but please note that doing so may affect the performance of our site.

4. Data Security

We take the protection of your personal data seriously. We implement a variety of security measures to ensure that your information is safe from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of online transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data with third-party service providers who assist us with website functionality and services, such as email marketing tools. These third parties are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not allowed to use your data for their own purposes.

We may also share your information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you wish to opt out of receiving newsletters or marketing communications, you can do so by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email we send.

If you wish to exercise any of your rights or have any questions about your data, please contact us at info@neoausguide.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised policy on this page with the updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

9. Contact Us
